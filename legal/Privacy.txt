Vex Email Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2/25/2025

Introduction:
Welcome to Vex Email ("Vex," "vex.email," "we," "us," or "our"). Your privacy is
important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and
protect your personal information when you use our email services.

Information We Collect:
When you create an account with Vex Email, we collect the following personal
information:

* First and Last Name
* Birthday
* Phone Number
* Alternate Email Address

How We Use Your Information:
The personal information you provide is used for the following purposes:

* Account Recovery - To verify your identity in case you need to recover access
to your account.
* Account Verification - To help ensure accounts are created by real users and
not automated bots.

Data Storage and Security:
By creating an account and submitting this information, you consent to it being
securely stored on Vex Email servers. Your information is never shared, sold, or
given to any third party. However, in certain cases, our support team may
manually review your data for the purposes outlined above. User emails are never
accessed by Vex or any third party.

Account Deletion:
At the user's request, accounts along with all personal data related to them can
be deleted.

Your Consent:
By signing up for Vex Email, you acknowledge and agree that your personal
information will be stored on our servers and may be reviewed manually by our
support team if necessary for account verification or recovery.

Changes to This Policy:
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted
on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us:
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@vex.email
